Common Questions

Before You Write

Our retreat center is located in India in a serene, private natural setting. We share the full address, detailed directions, and local travel tips after your booking is confirmed — this protects the privacy and sanctity of the space for all participants.

We recommend reviewing our Retreats page to compare durations and modules. If you're still unsure, please contact us to schedule a complimentary 20-minute orientation call — our team will help you choose the best fit for your goals, schedule, and budget.

All programs are taught in English. Translation support can be arranged with advance notice for Arabic, French, German, Spanish, Hindi, and several other languages — subject to availability and fees. Contact us to discuss your specific language needs.

Our retreat center is a dedicated healing space requiring all on-site guests to participate in the program. Non-participating family members are welcome to stay at nearby hotels and join for optional excursions or family dinners where available.

Our standard structure is 50% deposit at booking and 50% balance 30 days before your retreat. For the 21-Day Mastery we can accommodate a 3-installment plan on request. Contact our team to discuss your situation and we will do our best to find an arrangement that works.

Policies vary by program, but generally: full refund minus an admin fee for cancellations 45–90+ days before the retreat, and a full credit transfer within 30–45 days. Within 30 days of the retreat, a partial credit applies. We strongly recommend travel insurance. Contact us for the specific policy applicable to your chosen program.
